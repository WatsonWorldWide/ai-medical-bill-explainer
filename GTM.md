## 1. Executive Summary

The AI Medical Bill Explainer launches as a free, browser-based tool targeting US consumers who struggle to understand their medical bills. The GTM strategy prioritizes organic search and community-driven distribution to acquire users at zero cost during the MVP phase, with a path to monetization in V2 via a freemium model.

---

## 2. Market Opportunity

- Americans receive **3+ billion medical bills** annually
- Medical billing errors affect an estimated **80% of bills**, costing consumers billions
- Healthcare financial stress is a top-3 concern for US adults
- No dominant consumer-facing AI tool exists in this specific niche

---

## 3. Target Market

### Beachhead Market (MVP Focus)
US adults aged 30–65 who have received a confusing medical bill in the last 90 days. Estimated addressable population: **40M+ annually.**

### Expansion Markets (V2+)
- Caregivers managing bills for aging parents
- Patient advocacy groups
- HR departments offering it as an employee benefit

---

## 4. Positioning

**For** confused patients who don't understand their medical bills,  
**the AI Medical Bill Explainer is** a free, instant translation tool  
**that** turns complex billing codes and jargon into plain English,  
**unlike** calling your hospital's billing department or Googling codes one by one,  
**our product** gives you a complete, contextualized explanation in under 60 seconds — with zero data stored.

### Key Differentiators
- **Instant:** No waiting on hold, no appointment needed
- **Private:** Stateless — your bill data is never stored
- **Actionable:** Surfaces potential errors and gives you exact questions to ask
- **Free:** No account required for MVP

---

## 5. Pricing Strategy

### MVP: Fully Free
- No paywall, no account required
- Goal: maximize reach, gather feedback, validate retention

### V2: Freemium
| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 3 bill explanations/month, text input only |
| Plus | $4.99/mo | Unlimited explanations, PDF upload, saved history |
| Family | $9.99/mo | Up to 5 family members, shared history |

## 6. Distribution Channels

### Phase 1 — Organic (Months 1–3)
| Channel | Tactic | Expected Impact |
|---|---|---|
| SEO | Target keywords: "what does my medical bill mean", "medical billing codes explained", "how to read EOB" | Medium-term traffic driver |
| Reddit | Share in r/personalfinance, r/HealthInsurance, r/medicine | Early adopter acquisition |
| Product Hunt | Launch day post to drive initial spike | 500–2,000 day-one users |
| Twitter/X | Target threads about medical billing frustration | Awareness |

### Phase 2 — Partnerships (Months 4–6)
- Patient advocacy organizations (e.g., Patient Advocate Foundation)
- Personal finance newsletters and YouTube channels
- HR benefits platforms as a free employee tool

### Phase 3 — Paid (Month 6+, only if freemium converts)
- Google Search Ads on high-intent keywords
- Facebook/Instagram targeting 40–65 age group around open enrollment periods
